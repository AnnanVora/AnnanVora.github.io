Cultural Exchange Programs

These are the international cultural immersion experiences that I had in high school.

India x Spain

Colegio Internacional SEK-Ciudalcampo, Madrid

One of the most rewarding experiences of the past three years was this cultural exchange program to Spain. For me, it was the first time staying with a family of a completely different structure, culture, and language. The cultural difference started right from the first instant when I had to greet my host's mother with the traditional European kisses. This was something I had never done before and was new exposure to cultural differences. Such novel experiences continued through as I tried new foods and was introduced to new customs. I feel that this exchange was an extremely eye-opening experience and one that made me more open-minded and introduced me to different perspectives.

India x Czechia

Open Gate School, Prague

I hosted a Czech student for 2 weeks and was amazed to see how well he was able to adapt to the Indian culture, escpecially the food and family structure. He accompanied me to my grandparents' 50th anniversary celebration on his very first day in Mumbai and told me it was probably the biggest family celebration he had ever been to. This was an eye-opener for me, making me realize how unique each cultures is.
